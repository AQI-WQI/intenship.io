Smart Environmental Monitoring for a Sustainable Future

🌍 Problem Statement

Environmental pollution and poor water quality pose serious health and sustainability risks. Existing systems lack real-time monitoring and community-level awareness.

💡 Our Solution

We've built an IoT-based environmental monitoring system that provides real-time data on air and water quality. It uses sensors and ThingSpeak dashboards to visualize temperature, humidity, gas levels, and turbidity.

🚀 What Makes It Unique

  • ⚡ Real-time data tracking via ThingSpeak
  • 🌐 Web-based interface for public awareness
  • 📊 Clean dashboards for actionable insights
  • 🔗 Low-cost and easily deployable model
